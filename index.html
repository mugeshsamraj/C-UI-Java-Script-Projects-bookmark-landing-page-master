A Simple Bookmark
Manager

A clean and simple interface to organize your favourite
websites. Open a new browser tab and see your sites load
instantly. Try it for free.

Features

Our aim is to make it quick and easy for you to access your
favourite websites. Your bookmarks sync between your devices
so you can access them on the go.

  • Simple Bookmarking
  • Speedy Searching
  • Easy Sharing
Bookmark in one click Organize your bookmarks however you like. Our simple drag-and-drop interface gives you complete control over how you manage your favourite sites. More Info Intelligent search Our powerful search feature will help you find saved sites in no time at all. No need to trawl through all of your bookmarks.
